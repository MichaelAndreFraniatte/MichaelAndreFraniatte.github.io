Guide

Fortnite

Playing a Multiplayer Game Deserving It

The guide is made to understand better how to setup Fortnite security on console. There are less cheaters than Call of Duty, and it's playable on console using remote play, unlike this last. You will learn how to set your firewalls and to have good gameplay settings and graphisms. Let's see...

Firewall Settings

For remote play on your PC, you just need local ports 49152-65535 with distant port 443 in TCP, like for your console. For accessing servers you need local ports 49152-65535 with distant ports 5222-9222 in UDP for IP 14.0.0.0 to 34.0.0.0 with masks 255.0.0.0. You can set on your modem firewall local ports 49152-65535 with distant IP 16.0.0.0 and mask 240.0.0.0 with distant ports 5001-49151. You can only set it on your modem/router and disable Windows firewall on your PC.

Gameplay Settings

You can change the FOV (Field Of View) to maximum, you can disable some graphism features, you can let built layout for your controller because it's the most adapted. Change deadzone to lowest for right stick and a half for left stick, enable automatically open doors, enable automatically take on ground items, enable to put to right menu non reusing items, enable toggle aim with left button trigger, enable run automatically and always.

Audio and Social Settings

Disable microphone. Enable words checking. Enable to hide your name and name of others. Disable gifting. Disable to send your data. Disable all social features. On your console, disable every features for your account, but let enable multiplayer and crossplatform only.
