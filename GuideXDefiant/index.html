Guide

XDefiant

Playing a Multiplayer Free to Play Game

The guide is made to understand better how to setup XDefiant on console. There are less cheaters than Call of Duty, and it's playable on console using remote play, unlike this last. You will learn how to set your firewalls and to have good gameplay settings. Let's see...

Firewall Settings

For remote play on your PC, you just need local ports 49152-65535 with distant ports 80 and 443 in TCP, like for your console. For accessing servers you need all ports for your console. You can only set it on your modem/router and disable Windows firewall on your PC.

Gameplay Settings

You can change the FOV (Field Of View) to maximum if available, you can disable some graphism features, you can let built layout for your controller because it's the most adapted. Change deadzone to lowest for right stick and a half for left stick, set acceleration to 0 for aim, set multiplicators to 1 for aim, enable toggle aim with left button trigger.

Audio and Social Settings

Disable microphone. On your console, disable every features for your account, but let enable multiplayer and crossplatform only.
